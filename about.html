About Team Bharadwaj

Only All-Girls Team in SAE DDC

We are proud to be the only all-girls team to compete in the SAE Drone Design Challenge. Breaking stereotypes and building advanced drones independently, we set a strong precedent for diversity and engineering competence in the aeromodelling landscape.

Consistent Top 10 Placements

Through rigid design loops, flight tests, and well-documented reports, our team has consistently finished in the Top 10 ranks in SAE India DDC and other national challenges, securing multiple awards for aerodynamics design and structure innovations.

In-House Design and Fabrication

We believe in hands-on learning. From airfoil calculations (UIUC databases) and CAD modelling to wing hot-wire cutting, fuselage monokoting, and custom structural laser cutting, every single aspect of manufacturing is handled in our college laboratory by our members.
