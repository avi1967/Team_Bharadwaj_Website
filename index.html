Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize aeromodelling by crafting innovative fixed-wing aircraft, competing at the highest levels while fostering a culture of creativity and technical excellence.

Our Vision

To inspire and lead the next generation of aerospace innovators, building a legacy of knowledge, passion, and groundbreaking achievements in the skies.

Our Approach

We thrive on collaboration, with specialized sub-teams in aerodynamic design, CFD simulation, structural fabrication, avionics routing, and real-field flight testing working in sync to transform ideas into high-flying realities.

Our Impact

Through regional and national drone competitions, we empower students, advance local UAV research, and leave a lasting mark on the collegiate aerospace community.
